Our Story

Soulful. Southern. Comfort.

We started with a simple idea: make food that feels like home and serve it with genuine hospitality. From first light in the bakery to the last plate at supper, Green’s is a promise to cook with care, honor our community, and keep the coffee warm.

How We Got Here

Southern is a way of life—subtle but strong, always polite, and always cooking for someone. Comfort is food that settles you, made from scratch and served with grace.

Like a lot of folks in kitchens, we started by washing dishes, watching, and learning. The first “real” line we worked was in a busy, high-standard house where timing mattered and every plate had a story. Eventually we brought that discipline home to Oak Ridge, trading white tablecloths for a warm counter, a hot grill, and friends who’d become regulars.

Years later, after a few restaurants and a lot of miles, we chose to get back to basics: homestyle plates, scratch-made sweets, and green beans done the right way. We opened in historic Jackson Square and never looked back.

“Do the simple things exceptionally well—every single day.”

Our Grill — For the Love of Wood

We once catered an outdoor event and were asked to cook over real wood. We drove out to an orange grove, loaded the truck, and fired the grill. The flavor floored us—clean, deep, and undeniably Southern.

Since that day we’ve chased honest wood-fired heat. It takes more time and attention, and it’s worth every minute. Wood-grilled corn, asparagus, squash, fish, poultry, and pork ribs—the smoke is gentle, the sear is right, and the results speak for themselves.

More time. More effort. Better quality. After a while, it just becomes our way of doing things.

Community Involvement

We’re a community-rooted restaurant. From day one we’ve supported local booster clubs, youth programs, United Way partners, and neighborhood events across Oak Ridge.

Each year we rally our guests to raise funds for the Oak Ridge Animal Shelter and other local causes—bake-sale desserts, donation nights, and a lot of good conversation. None of it happens without our loyal customers, and we’re grateful.
